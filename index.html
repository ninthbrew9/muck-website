What we do

Property Maintenance Services

Pressure Washing

Ireland's damp climate means driveways and patios collect moss, algae and grime year-round. Left untreated, moss becomes a serious slip hazard and works into paving joints — gradually lifting and staining the surface. A regular clean restores the look, protects your investment and keeps it safe underfoot.

Roof Cleaning

Moss spreads fast on Irish roofs due to our wet climate. Once established, it traps moisture against your tiles — causing cracking in cold snaps and cutting years off your roof's lifespan. Cleaning and treating every few years can prevent a very costly replacement and keep your home properly weatherproofed.

Gutter Cleaning

Blocked gutters are one of the most common — and most avoidable — causes of damp in Irish homes. When gutters overflow, water seeps into fascias, walls and foundations. An annual clean keeps water flowing away from your home and prevents the kind of structural damage that costs thousands to fix.

Window Cleaning

Between the rain, road dirt and coastal salt air, Irish windows lose their clarity fast. Dirty glass lets in less light and leaves a poor impression on visitors and potential buyers alike. Regular cleaning removes grime before it stains permanently — keeping your home bright and looking its best all year round.
